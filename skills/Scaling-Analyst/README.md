# Scaling Analyst

The Scaling Analyst is the synthesis and evidence-assessment component of Scaling Intelligence.

It works from curated repository materials produced by the Scaling Librarian.

## Core question

> What does the repository collectively mean?

## It is designed to

- synthesize across methodologies and cases;
- identify recurring concepts and heuristics;
- compare analytical approaches;
- assess evidence strength;
- surface methodological tensions;
- evaluate capability coverage;
- identify knowledge gaps; and
- recommend research and repository priorities.

## It is not designed to

- ingest primary documents;
- replace original sources;
- curate metadata;
- modify Knowledge Cards;
- coach a specific team directly; or
- make final stage-gate or funding decisions.

## Main outputs

- Insight Pages
- Comparative Analyses
- Capability Reviews
- Research Notes

## Dependencies

The Skill performs best when supplied with:

- Knowledge Cards;
- Concept Pages;
- Heuristic Pages;
- Evidence Records;
- the Capability Framework; and
- an Open Questions Log.
